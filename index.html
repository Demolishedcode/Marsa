Who are we?

Hi Welcome to Marsa!

We are Sander and Marijn. Two students from OBC Bemmel and the founders of Marsa. Havo 5, the exam year in Holland, was coming up and we had to make a personal project. Both of us are really into programming, and so the base of the plan was set: code some kind of software. But what should we program and what is achievable in a few months? Than we came up with Marsa.

Marsa is a software with helps you organizing your personal life. It contains multiple groups with different features to suit your needs. A Calender will be the middle of every group, in this calender you can plan activities and the whole group will be able to see what is going on.

If there are any questions about the product feel free to contact us
